## Design Decisions

1. **Step Flow + Split View combination**: Step Flow shows "how VQE works" (conceptual), SplitView shows "what's happening now" (state). Together they tell the complete story.
2. **Color coding**: Blue=Classical, Purple=Quantum. Visual domain separation without needing physical separator lines.
3. **Section order**: Comparison as anchor (results first), then concept (flow), then detail (split view), then evidence (chart).
4. **Snapshot circuit in SplitView, final circuit in CircuitDisplay**: Avoids duplication while showing evolution and final state.
