## Changes Made

### 1. Backend trace endpoint (/api/dj/trace/<case_id>)

Fixed stages count from 6 to 5:
- Step 1: Inisialisasi awal έΗΤ q: |0έθσ... , ancilla: |0έθσ
- Step 2: H pada q + X pada ancilla έΗΤ q: |+έθσ... , ancilla: |1έθσ
- Step 3: H pada ancilla έΗΤ q: |+έθσ... , ancilla: |έΙΤέθσ
- Step 4: Oracle $U_f$ έΗΤ q: |+έθσ... , ancilla: |έΙΤέθσ
- Step 5: Measurement έΗΤ q: |basisέθσ , ancilla: -

Dirac notation used throughout.

### 2. Frontend table component

Changed to parse concatenated Dirac strings:
- Input: concatenated string "|0έθσ|0έθσ|0έθσ"
- Parser: split by 3-char tokens

### 3. Partition spans

4 partitions: init, prep, oracle, measure (not 5)
